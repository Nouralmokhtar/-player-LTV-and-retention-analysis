# player LTV and retention analysis
Why this Matters?
Used by CRM, Marketing, and Finance to decide:
	•	Who gets bonuses
	•	Who is likely to churn
	•	How much a player is worth
  

 The Business Question will be:
How valuable is each player over time, and how does retention evolve after the first bet?

an Assumptions:
- One player can place multiple bets and deposits
- GGR = bet_amount - win_amount
- Retention is measured by betting activity after first bet

 Key KPIs for this Case:
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Gross Gaming Revenue (GGR)
- Day 7 / Day 30 Retention Rate

Insights This Analysis Enables
- Identify high-value players for VIP programs
- Detect early churn behavior
- Optimize CRM targeting and bonus allocation
